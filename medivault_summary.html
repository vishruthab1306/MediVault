MediVault: AI powered reports scanner and personal Health locker

An intelligent, secure personal health information system combining an advanced mobile frontend with real-time Generative AI medical document parsing and automated vital telemetry tracking.

1. Brief Overview

MediVault is designed to solve the problem of fragmented and complex clinical documentation. It provides a personal medical locker that allows patients to snap photos of physical clinical documents and immediately parse them into structured, trackable health profiles in real time. It serves as a unified baseline for critical medical metrics, chronic diagnoses, and consulting references.

2. Core Features

  • Real-Time Multimodal Camera Scanner: Captures high-fidelity photos of medical reports directly inside the app, uploading compressed base64 images securely to the backend.
  • Multimodal Visual OCR Engine: Feeds photo assets directly to Google Gemini 2.0 Flash to perform deep document text extraction, completely bypassing the need for native offline OCR libraries.
  • AI Clinical Summaries: Generates automated, patient-friendly, easy-to-read clinical explanations under a read-only lock to preserve data integrity.
  • Profile Smart-Sync Updates: Parses critical laboratory findings (blood glucose, blood pressure, pulse, chronic conditions, and allergies) and automatically updates the patient's master clinical baseline.
  • Telemetry Metrics & Trends: Automatically logs extracted vital metrics into SQLite database tables on the report date, generating dynamic 90-day tracking logs.

Authentication Architecture & Sandbox Pattern
To support rapid, deterministic sandbox testing, a Single-User Sandbox Pattern was engineered. During registration or login, the database truncates all SQLite tables to provide a fresh canvas. For production environments, this will transition to dynamic UUIDv4 keys, bcrypt password salting (minimum 10 rounds), and secure signed JSON Web Tokens (JWT) passed in client Authorization headers.

4. Future Roadmap Ideas

  • Edge-AI Local Scanning: Integrate local on-device LLMs (such as Gemini Nano) to execute visual OCR directly on-device without requiring network availability.
  • Health Ecosystem Synchronization: Build native bridges to automatically synchronize parsed vital telemetry with Apple HealthKit and Google Fit databases.
  • Paramedic Offline QR Access: Create locked-screen emergency QR code widgets containing encrypted key baseline metrics that first responders can scan offline instantly.
